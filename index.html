Hi. I'm Naaz Nagori.

Graduate Teaching Assistant & IEEE Chair at Clark University

I am a passionate technology enthusiast with a background in deep learning, machine learning, and embedded systems. Currently, I serve as the Chair of the IEEE student chapter at Clark University, where I work with fellow students and faculty to foster innovation and technical growth. I have hands-on experience in Android/AGL/QNX kernel development, as well as expertise in GPU data capture and real-time application development. My research and professional work focus on improving user experience in mobile gaming and healthcare through advanced machine learning algorithms. I am always eager to explore new challenges and contribute to meaningful tech-driven solutions.
